Where do you find yourself?

Posted by Vrinda Kathpal on March 13, 2021

Where do you find yourself?

Not in the city that you stay in or the country that you live in or the world to which you belong

But the moments in which you find yourself and you realise that you exist.

What if the life you have is more then just a mere tool of survival for you to endure and comprise of jealousy and turmoil and hatred?

What if you existed not to find why you exist but to find the where the when and the how?

To find the places where the roses closed for everyone but bloomed for you in the farthest corner of the earth where the living and the dead seemed to communicate via the undiscovered colours of the air and talked about you....

To find the roads on which you want to get lost to and never be found or maybe to look for maps to just be found in some small city of your heart by yourself.....

To find the moments in the day that don’t comprise of the seconds in the minutes of the hours in the day but the smile on your face and the peace in your eyes....

To dine on the speciality of the sunlight and look for the hidden moon in the clouds to give you the feeling of existing when nobody even knows your name ....

To make you believe in the illusion of reality or the magical mistake of breathing or maybe just to keep tracks of the breaths gone by rather then those that are to come or maybe just to exhale and inhale for a while......

To make you devour the innermost madness of your soul that nobody has ever seen and display it all in a theatrical portrayal of your own life or maybe just watch someone else’s come to life this time ......

To make you forgive the unbecoming advances of time that don’t seem to stop for anyone yet you forget the way it forms and transforms when you don’t remember what you were doing anyway .....

To make you hear yourself when you are filled with gossips and whispers of the ghosts of everyones pasts apparently and just seem to ignore your own hearts cry or maybe just to remind you that you have a voice even if you haven’t heard it in a while .....

Or maybe just wondering or imagining or fantasising or typing or doodling this all out on a blank piece of paper is what makes you exist not in the mind of others but in your own life where you live that you so easily forgot to create and then just wonder again and again and again why you so seemingly forget to exist for yourself......
