The path leading to tomorrow

Posted by Vrinda Kathpal on March 13, 2021

This path that I walk on that a hundred other souls have trodden about,

Do you think they see the same that I do, is watching and perceiving the same thats my only doubt ?

Do you think they notice the changes in the colour of the sky ?

When the sun is waiting to say goodbye

The red hues melting down into the pinkish tones of light

The blue sky in the middle merging with the yellow light at the end of sight

The unencumbered vines of leaves that lay on one side of that road

That wisdomous oak tree bending over like looking for something he forgot

That dead end road where only forgotten hearts go to heal

The sound of each step and the crunching of the leaves under your feet

The one eyed monster waiting in that one corner with no light on

Where the two roads hold hands whispering if anyone would ever come

That one hole from the fence where the sun kisses your cheeks perfectly

Do you think they see the innocence of the rays behind their sun kissed selfie

The canopy of flowers all down on the ground in full bloom

That one tall tree that stands apart from everyone else

With yellow bright flowers on the top branches settled in like sprinkles on that ice cream melt

The trodden yet forgotten path that holds a thousand passerbys

All eager to reach the end and jump over the wall but never wondering to stay a while
